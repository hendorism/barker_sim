debarker-sim works best in Chrome.

zoom in with your browser till it fills the window. you can even full screen your browser to eliminate distractions.

This is the debarker simulator to teach you to run the barker. The barker is easy to run, but it requires sustained concentration and focus.

To begin, press the left foot switch pedal to run the infeed deck. This will cause the logs on the deck to move toward the infeed trough on the north side of the infeed deck.
